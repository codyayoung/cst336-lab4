Jupiter

One of the gas giants along with Saturn, Jupiter's mass is 2.5 times that of all other planets in the solar system combined.
It is composed mainly of hydrogen, along with helium. The Great Red Spot is a giant storm currently raging on the planet, and is one of its characteristic features.
It has been known to exist since at least the 17th century.
